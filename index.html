The Daily News

Our cigar. Our story. Now all over the country.

The Daily News was blended by our friend Z at Córdoba & Morales — it started life as his event cigar. Z would set up at events and roll right in front of customers, and that cigar he was rolling was something special.

We noticed. So we did what any good regulars would do: we pestered him. Relentlessly. For a while. Until he finally gave in and agreed to start selling it to us at Jimmy's.

That's when word got out. Now the Daily News is carried in shops all over the country — and we couldn't be prouder of Z for what he built. We're just glad we were annoying enough to make it happen. We keep the freshest stock right here, where it first hit a shelf.

Daily News — Connecticut  Toro
Mild-Medium Creamy Cedar

The smoothest of the three. A natural Connecticut shade wrapper gives it an easy, creamy draw that's great any time of day — especially if you want something you can smoke start to finish without slowing down.

Daily News — Habano  Toro
Medium Pepper Earthy

A Habano wrapper brings a little more body and a touch of spice to Z's blend. This one has some kick to it without going full throttle — a great middle ground for daily smokers who want a bit more going on.

Daily News — Maduro  Toro
Medium-Full Dark Chocolate Sweet Finish

The darkest and boldest of the three. The maduro wrapper adds a natural sweetness and a rich, dark complexity that rounds out beautifully by the last third. If you like a full-flavored smoke, start here.
